Folder untuk gambar banner parallax (hanya background, bergerak saat scroll).

- between_about_mission: antara section About dan Vision & Mission.
  Isi di config: parallax_banners.between_about_mission => 'images/parallax/banner-1.jpg'

- between_unified_values: antara Unified Security dan Our Values.
  Isi di config: parallax_banners.between_unified_values => 'images/parallax/banner-2.jpg'

Rasio disarankan landscape (lebar > tinggi). Tinggi section: h-48 (mobile), h-64 (md), h-80 (lg).
