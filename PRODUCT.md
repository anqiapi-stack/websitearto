# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Government, defense, and strategic enterprise decision-makers in Indonesia seeking secure IT integration and surveillance solutions. They visit the site to evaluate the company's capabilities, verify its partnerships, and check its solutions for national vital infrastructures.

## Product Purpose
To showcase PT. Artomoro Prima Internasional's profile, core solutions, mission/vision, values, and strategic alliances. The site builds institutional trust and provides contact channels for partnership and solution inquiries.

## Brand Personality
Traditional, corporate, conservative, and institutional. The voice is stable, secure, authoritative, and highly professional, reflecting the high-trust defense and government sectors it serves.

## Anti-references
Avoid flat, uninspired corporate templates that use card grids and side-stripe borders repetitively. Do not use generic high-tech neon details or video-game/sci-fi military HUD aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. **Institutional Authority**: Project stability, security, and high trust through strong layouts, classical alignments, and robust typography.
2. **Dynamic Dignity**: Avoid flat, static templates by using rich visual rhythm, subtle hover responses, and smooth parallax backdrops while keeping the overall feel conservative.
3. **Structured Clarity**: Group and present complex defense system integrations in a clear, digestible layout that feels cohesive rather than fragmented.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure standard responsiveness across mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports, prioritizing clear typographic contrast and legible sizing.
